--- layout: page title: "Research Projects" permalink: /projects/ --- # Research Projects ## Project 1: Parsing Difficulty Simulation & ANOVA (Python) **Description**: This mini-project simulates human sentence processing difficulty based on syntactic structure. It is inspired by classic psycholinguistic research and uses statistical analysis to compare simulated reading times for different sentence types.. **Excerpt**: This project explores how different sentence structures affect how quickly people can read and understand sentences. It compares three types of sentences: SVO (Subject-Verb-Object), Passive, and OSV (Object-Subject-Verb). By using statistical analysis (ANOVA) and data visualization (boxplots), the project checks if there are significant differences in reading times between these sentence types. The results help us better understand how sentence structure influences reading and comprehension. **GitHub Repository**: [Parsing Difficulty Project - GitHub]([https://github.com/yourusername/word-order-project](https://github.com/katiz1/parsing-difficulty-project)) ---
